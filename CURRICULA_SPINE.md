## Level 2 — A2 (4 units). Source: `s2u1–s2u4`. Status: spine extracted ✅, authoring pending.

### Unit 1 — "Getting Around" (src title: *How often do you play?*)
Theme arc: technology, directions, daily tech habits, abilities, what's happening now.
1. **Directions** — Grammar: *imperatives / giving directions*. Vocab: public places, prepositions of movement. (read: how to get somewhere)
2. **Tech habits** — Grammar: *present simple + Wh- questions + adverbs of frequency*. Vocab: cell phones, electronic devices. (speaking: how often you do things)
3. **Abilities** — Grammar: *modal `can` (ability & possibility)*. Vocab: leisure activities, abilities.
4. **Right now** — Grammar: *present continuous (yes/no questions)*. Vocab: vacation activities.
5. **What are they wearing?** — Grammar: *present continuous (Wh- questions)*. Vocab: clothes, action verbs.

### Unit 2 — "Plans & Plenty" (src title: *Are you going to travel…?*)
Theme arc: future plans/predictions, food & quantity, shopping.
1. **Plans** — Grammar: *`going to` (future) / predictions from evidence*. Vocab: work & leisure, possessions.
2. **Predictions** — Grammar: *`will` / `won't` (promises & predictions)*. Vocab: (light-hearted) future fun.
3. **Would you like…?** — Grammar: *countable & uncountable nouns / `would like to`*. Vocab: food, meals, measurements.
4. **Shopping list** — Grammar: *quantifiers `some / any / a few / a little` + `there is / there are`*. Vocab: food, containers, measurements.
5. **How much is it?** — Grammar: *`how much / how many` + definite & indefinite articles*. Vocab: supermarket sections, prices.

### Unit 3 — "Back Then" (src title: *Were you at home yesterday?*)
Theme arc: how/where, people, the past (to be → simple past).
1. **How & where** — Grammar: *adverbs of place & manner / purpose clauses (to + verb)*. Vocab: means of transportation.
2. **Him & her** — Grammar: *subject & object pronouns*. Vocab: relationships.
3. **Were you there?** — Grammar: *verb `to be` past (was / were)*. Vocab: movies, past time expressions.
4. **Did he invent it?** — Grammar: *simple past — regular verbs*. Vocab: inventors, scientists.
5. **Did you do it?** — Grammar: *simple past — questions & short answers*. Vocab: celebrations.

### Unit 4 — "When It Happened" (src title: *When did that happen?*)
Theme arc: the past in depth (irregulars, used to, duration, time markers).
1. **What did you do?** — Grammar: *simple past — regular & irregular verbs*. Vocab: past habits.
2. **Used to (do)** — Grammar: *`used to` (past habits)*. Vocab: past states & habits.
3. **Used to (be)** — Grammar: *`used to` (past states)*. Vocab: how people/places used to be.
4. **How long did it last?** — Grammar: *simple past — duration*. Vocab: prehistoric animals, past time expressions.
5. **Last & ago** — Grammar: *simple past — `last` and `ago`*. Vocab: action verbs, weekend events.
